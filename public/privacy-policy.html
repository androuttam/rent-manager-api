This Privacy Policy explains how the Rent Manager application ("the App") collects, uses, stores and protects information. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are

The App is operated by [YOUR NAME / BUSINESS NAME], located at [YOUR CITY, INDIA]. For any privacy related question you can contact us at [YOUR-EMAIL@example.com].

2. Information we collect

The App is used by property owners to manage their rented flats and tenants. We collect:

  • Account information: mobile number and an encrypted password used to sign in.
  • Tenant records entered by the owner: tenant name, mobile number, vehicle details, flat details, agreed rent, move-in date and status.
  • Documents and photos uploaded by the owner: tenant photograph, rent agreement and identity proof documents.
  • Financial records: rent payments, expenses and electricity bill entries created inside the App.
  • App preferences: selected language and login session token stored on the device.

We do not collect location data, contacts, SMS, call logs or advertising identifiers.

3. How the information is used

  • To authenticate users and keep each owner's data separate and private.
  • To display rent, expense, electricity and pending-payment reports inside the App.
  • To generate PDF reports and receipts that the user chooses to share or download.
  • To operate, maintain and improve the App.

We never sell, rent or trade personal information to any third party.

4. Where the data is stored

Application data is stored in a MongoDB Atlas database and uploaded files (photos, agreements, identity documents) are stored on Cloudinary. Both are reputable third-party cloud service providers and the data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are stored only in a hashed (irreversible) form.

5. Responsibility of the owner user

Tenant information is entered into the App by the property owner. The owner is responsible for obtaining the tenant's consent before uploading the tenant's personal details, photograph or identity documents, and for using that information only for legitimate rental record-keeping purposes.

6. Data sharing

We do not share personal information with anyone except: (a) the cloud service providers listed above who process data on our behalf, and (b) where disclosure is required by applicable law or a valid legal request.

7. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained for as long as the account remains active. A user may request deletion of their account and all associated data by writing to [YOUR-EMAIL@example.com]. Such requests are normally completed within 30 days.

8. Children

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

9. Security

We use HTTPS transport encryption, hashed passwords and token-based access control. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
